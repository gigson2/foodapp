# Customer Dashboard And Brand Sync

The customer experience is tied to live company data and authenticated customer data.

## Brand synchronization

- browser title is updated from company settings
- favicon can be updated from company settings
- storefront and customer-facing copy use database-backed business details where available
- shared footer branding uses the current year automatically

## Customer portal areas

- overview dashboard
- orders
- notifications
- reviews
- profile

## Customer metrics

The customer dashboard reports:

- total orders
- active orders
- completed orders
- cancelled orders
- total spent
- pending reviews
- unread notifications

## Push readiness

- authenticated customers can save browser push subscriptions
- notification permission state is tracked on the frontend
- customer notification flows can use both in-app and web-push delivery
